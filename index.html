McGill University

Throughout my time at McGill, I switched programs a couple of times before finally settling on Earth System Science. The program was a muilti disciplinary Earth Sciences program that taught us the interconnectivity of all of Earth's components (i.e. Biosphere, cryosphere, etc.). We were also taught how to code entire coupled models to mimic these systems numerically.

I acheived a great deal at McGill University. Sure there were a couple of awards and some scholarships but, I am talking about more narrowing down my interests.
